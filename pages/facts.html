It is illegal to leave the house without underwear in Bangkok.

The name Koh Yao Yai means "big long island"

There is so little rain on the island of Santorini, that wine is more plentiful than water

It takes roughly 3 and a half hours by car to cross the island from east to west, and around 2 and a half from north to south
