Facts


Bangkok, Thailand

On the other side of the globe, you will find Thailand's capital city, Bangkok. Along with being Thailand's capital, Bangkok is also the country's most populous city, filled with beautiful temples and palaces, canals, and busy markets.

Berlin, Germany

The capital city of Germany lives up to its name. Berlin is the most populous city in the country and is entwined with history all around. Just by taking a short walk around the city centre, you will be exposed to different representations of German historic periods.

Grand Cayman, Cayman Islands

The Cayman Islands are a group of breath-takingly beautiful islands located in the Carribean Sea, not too far from Cuba. The largest of the three islands, Grand Cayman, provides a perfect santuary to unwind, relax, and enjoy the sun.

Chiang Mai, Thailand

Chiang Mai takes you away from the city and island life of Thailand and into the mountains of the beautiful country. Chiang Mai is the largest city in northern Thailand and is known for the beautiful mountains and lush countryside that surrounds the area.

Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Dubai is one of the seven emirates that makes up the United Arab Emirates. This emirate is the most modern and progressive of the seven, developing at an extreme rate for both tourists and traders.

Ha Long Bay, Vietnam

Ha Long Bay is located in northern Vietnam, east of the country's capital city-Hanoi. Ha Long Bay is located in the area of the Gulf of Tangkia and is known for its oceanic topography. It is often included in the Seven Wonders of the World.

Jersulam, Israel

Jerusalem is the cultural, spiritual, and national center for the Jewish state, as well as being a holy city to Islamic and Christian populations This city is one of the oldest and holiest in the world, containing some of the sites of Christian Biblic stories and located not too far from Jesus' birthplace, Bethelehem.

Koh Yao Yai, Thailand

Just a quick boat ride from Thailand's more popular island city-Phuket-Koh Yao Yai is a group of islands known for its natural beauty and preservation from human degradation. On this island you can enjoy all of the beauty of Phuket, but in a more secluded and relaxing manor.

Lake Como, Italy

In the northern region of Italy, Lake Como has been a popular tourist destination for over 100 years. The area is distinguished for its fresh air, water, mountains, and good weather.

London, England

Across the pound, in the United Kingdom, is England's capital and largest city. London or "Europe's Manhattan" is a noisy, vibrant, multicultural, and modern city, with little reminders of the country's history sprinkled throughout the metropolis.

Mae Hon Song, Thailand

In the northern region of Thailand you will find the small and quaint town of Mae Hon Song. In this city you might find yourself visiting the local villagers, walking across the Su Tong Pae Bamboo Bridge, or visiting the mud spa.

Nairobi, Kenya

Situated on the Nairobi River, the city is not only the largest and fastest growing city in Kenya, but also in Africa. A couple hours drive from the city, you might find yourself faced with a lion or a family of elephants on an esteemed African safari in the Masai Mara National Wildlife Reserve.

Paris, France

Paris is the cosmopolitan capital of France and one of the largest city's in Europe. Paris earns it's labels as "The City of Light" and "The Capital of Fashion" with its vastly influential culture, art, fashion, food and design, as well as its beautiful scenery and romantic atmosphere.

Porto, Portugal

Porto is Portugal's second largest city and capital of the Northern Region. The city was built along the hills in order to overloo the Douro River estuary.

Prague, Czech Republic

Along with being the capital city and largest city of the Czech Republic, Prague is famous for its unique medieval architecture. With magical bridges, cathedrals, and gold-tipped towers and church domes, the city is thriving in its beauty and history.

Rome, Italy

Rome, or the "Eternal City", is the largest city in Italy as well as the country's capital. Upon walking the cobblestone streets of the great city, you may happen upon the Colosseum, Trevi Fountain, or Pantheon, or possibly even wonder to Vatican City and have a run in with the Pope.

Santorini, Greece

Santorini is a volcani island in the Cyclades group of the Greek Islands. It is famous for its dramatic views, beautiful sunsets, and dominant white architecture.

Castellemare del Golfo, Sicily

Castellemare del Golfo is a beautiful coastal town on the rugged and attractive island of Sicily. In this island town, you can take a scenic boat ride to different coastal cities of Sicily, take a drive up Mount Etna, and find yourself snacking on some of the best food in Italy.

Venice, Italy

Venice is one of the most interesting and lovely places in the world. It is the "City of water" or the "Sanctuary on a Lagoon". In Venice, instead of hailing a cab, you hail a gondola. The city is surrounded by water, but does not lack in its beauty and extraordinary artwork and architecture.

Vienna, Austria

Vienna is the capital of Austria as well as the largest city in Austria by far. Vienna is Austria's cultural, economic, and political centre. Packed full of concert halls and stages for performances that range from classical to jazz, Vienna was dubbed "The City of Music".

Zanzibar, Tanzania

Zanzibar is a quiet island located off the coast of Tanzania. This small island is the perfect place to get off the grid for a relaxing couple of days and enjoy the scenic ocean, untouched beaches, and magnificent sunsets.
